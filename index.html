ABOUT US

With the dream to explore the world of Humanoid Robotics and the realization of lack of education in the field of robotics, SRM Team Humanoid was formed in September 2013 by Harikrishnan L. with support from SRM University.

Our Vision: We strive toward excellence to put India as a forerunner in the field of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission: To successfully represent India at one of the most coveted Humanoid Robotics competition, the RoboCup International.

DOMAINS

Coding

Work with mind numbing algorithms and indigenous packages to bring life to our robots.

Mechanical

Engineers come up with their own original design using new technology. Thereafter, fabricate and assemble a full sized model which provides the backbone of our robots.

Computer Vision

Experiment with different algorithms and indigenous packages to give the gift of vision to our robots.

Corporate

Procurement of components, contacting sponsors, working out logistics, documentation and inventory management are some of their responsibilities.

Electronics

Electrical and Electronics engineers collaborate together to find the best and most efficient power source, processing units and wired/wireless communication technologies for the robots.

Control

Engineers come up with awesome equations to provide robust and dynamic balance to the robots.
