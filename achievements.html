OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

RoboGames 2018

7 members of the team represented the country in the 14th International RoboOlympics, RoboGames 2018 in Pleasanton, California. They secured a total of 3 medals in various events. They bagged a bronze medal in BIPED RACE event and also won two gold medals for our team, one in the ARTBOT: MUSICAL event and other in AUTONOMOUS OBSTACLE AVOIDING CHALLENGE

IEEE International Conference 2017

The 5th Humanoid Robot Application Challenge was held from the 24th-28th of September at the 2017 IEEE International Conference in Vancouver, Canada. The theme of the humanoid application challenge was robot magic. Our team presented a trick where the robot can recognize and manipulate a die to ensure that a card signed by a volunteer is picked last. Four of the team's members represented our team and won the 3rd Place in the competition.

BIT ATMOS'17

ATMOS is the annual techno-management festival of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus and 4 of our team members represented the University and secured 1st position in the Tech-Expo showcasing Sniper Ghost Bot.

RoboGames 2017

8 members of the team represented the country in the 13th International RoboOlympics, RoboGames 2017 in Pleasanton, California. They won a total of 4 medals in various fields. They bagged a bronze medal in the HUMANOID SUMO R/C event and also won TWO SILVER medal for HUMANOID FREESTYLE ORIGINAL EVENT and also in the BIPED RACE EVENT. They won a GOLD for the nation in the PENALTY KICK EVENT.

BITS Atmos'16

Atmos is the annual techno-management fest of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus and & 7 members of our team represented our college and secured 2nd position in Tech-Expo and also won the Best INNOVATION AWARD by WAT Media Pvt. Ltd.by showcasing A.T. Hum.

IIT Guwahati Techniche'16

Techniche is the flagship tech fest of IIT Guwahati. Our team of 9 members represented the University in TECHNICHE 2016. The team secured the GOLD medal for the Phototron event in the fest.

Robogames 2016

9 members of SRM Team Humanoid represented the country in the 12th International RoboOlympics, Robogames’16 held in Pleasonton, California and secured the consolation prize for Humanoid Freestyle Original Event.

INDO-US Robo League'15

iURL is an international level competition organized by Technophilia Systems in association with RACi USA. An 8 membered team participated at the national round of iURL at IIT BOMBAY on 28-29th March 2015. The team came up with the prodigious result during the event and secured SECOND position with a record of 3 minutes and 20 block.

RoboGames 2015

Robogames is the world’s largest open robotics competition. 9 members of the team represented the country in the 11th International RoboOlympics, Robogames’15 in San Mateo, California and bagged the bronze medel in it’s first attempt in the Humanoid Freestyle Original Event.

Bazaar’13

The Bazaar - Retail Shop Expo’13 was conducted by the SRM School Of Management. The team won the Best Innovation award at Bazaar’13 Tech Expo.
