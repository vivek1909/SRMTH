OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

RoboGames is the Olympics of Robots where the best minds from around the world come to compete in the field of robotics. Our team represented India in the field of Humanoid robotics. A new team of 7 members participated in the event and proved their worth to the world by winning. Srm team humanoid secured 3 medals - Gold medal in Artbot: Musical Gold medal in Autonomous Obstacle Avoiding Challenge Bronze medal in Biped Race
