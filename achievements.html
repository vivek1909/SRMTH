OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

RoboGames 2018

RoboGames is the Olympics of Robots where the best minds from around the world come to compete in the field of robotics. Our team represented India in the field of Humanoid robotics. A new team of 7 members participated in the event and proved their worth to the world by winning. Srm team humanoid secured 3 medals - Gold medal in Artbot: Musical Gold medal in Autonomous Obstacle Avoiding Challenge Bronze medal in Biped Race.

Third Place at 2017 IEEE International Conference

The 5th Humanoid Robot Application Challenge was held from the 24th to 28th of September at the 2017 IEEE International Conference for Robotic Systems in Vancouver, Canada. In 2017, the theme of the humanoid application challenge was robot magic. That is the use of a humanoid robot as either a magician, an assistant, and/or as a prop during the performance of a magic trick. For example, the robot may help during a card trick or read the mind of the audience. Our team presented a trick where the robot can recognize and manipulate a die to ensure that a card signed by a volunteer is picked last. Four of the team's members represented our team and won the 3rd Place in the competition.

FIRST position in Tech Expo at BIT ATMOS'17

ATMOS is the annual techno-management festival of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus and 4 of our team members represented the University and secured 1st position in the Tech-Expo showcasing Sniper Ghost Bot.
